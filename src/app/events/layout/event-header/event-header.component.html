DevFest 2023: Coming soon!

DevFests are local conferences hosted by Google Developer Groups (GDG) around the world. Each DevFest is crafted by its local organizers to fit the needs and interests of its local developer community. Whether it be through hands-on learning experiences, technical talks delivered in local languages by experts, or by simply meeting fellow local developers, DevFest attendees learn how to build together and innovate on Google's developer tools.

DevFest opens for registration on October 2, 2023. Check back for updates!
