About

GDG Chandigarh

Google Developer Group Chandigarh (GDG Chandigarh ) is a young community of developers with the motive by the developers for the developers and from developers.

In short its a community of developers who are interested in Google's developer technologies from the Android, Chrome, Drive, and Google Cloud platforms, to product APIs like the Cast API, Maps API, YouTube API and Many more.

"Bringing people with interest in technology together since 2018"
